Custom Keyboard and Controllers

This keyboard is a KBDfans KBD67 Lite R2 I obtained from a group buy over three years ago. I wanted it because it was a relatively inexpensive gasket-mounted keyboard. I put Gateron milky black switches(lubed and filmed) in it. The keycap set is GMK Synthwave that I did not get from a group buy.

Gamecube Controllers

This is my Gamecube controller. It is themed to look like EVA Unit 01 from Neon Genesis Evangelion. I chose to include a thumbstick from a Wii nunchuck for looks and because the original thumbstick kept on hurting my thumb while playing Super Smash Bros Ultimate(Peach requires a lot of movement).

I resin casted these buttons, which involved creating a silicon mold of the original buttons and curing them in a vacuum chamber.

I wrapped the cable in green paracord. This involved desoldering and resoldering the 6 individual wires on the controller PCB. This was my first experience with soldering.
